Hospitality Revenue Insights Dashboard – AtliQ Grands

## Objective
Provide actionable insights to the Revenue Management Team of AtliQ Grands (a luxury hotel chain) to help improve revenue and regain market share using data analytics and business intelligence.

---

## 🏨 Problem Statement
AtliQ Grands, operating in the hospitality industry for 20 years, is facing a decline in market share and revenue due to increased competition and ineffective decision-making.

The goal of this project is to analyze historical data and generate insights that support better business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Hotel booking data  
- Revenue data  
- Occupancy rates  
- City and property-level performance  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Created
- Revenue  
- RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room)  
- ADR (Average Daily Rate)  
- Occupancy %  
- Booking trends  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Revenue trend analysis  
- City-wise and property-wise performance  
- Occupancy and booking insights  
- KPI tracking for business decisions  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Certain cities/properties are underperforming in revenue  
- Occupancy rates vary significantly across locations  
- Peak booking periods identified  
- Revenue loss linked to lower occupancy in key segments  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on improving occupancy in low-performing properties  
- Optimize pricing strategy during low-demand periods  
- Target high-performing cities for expansion  
- Improve marketing strategies for underperforming segments  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analytics and dashboards can help hospitality businesses make informed decisions and improve revenue performance.
